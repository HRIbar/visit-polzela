It is the oldest and best-preserved granary in Andraž. It is part of a farm complex that, along with all its buildings (house, barn, hayrack, double hayrack, and chapel), is registered as cultural heritage. It was built in 1810, with the year carved above the entrance, surrounded by beautifully stylized tulip flowers, and the names of the granary owner, Luka Jelovšek, and the builder, Marko Vodrih.
