In the settlement of Kopank in Podvin near Polzela stands the birthplace of Neža Maurer, a renowned Slovenian poet, writer, publicist, and honorary citizen of the Municipality of Polzela. The house was fully restored in 2022 and is one of the few preserved in its immediate and distant surroundings, telling the story of the way of life, farming, and living in the past. Neža Maurer's house is considered a cultural monument of local importance with the characteristics of an ethnological monument and invites visitors to take a tour. Under the nearby hayrack, benches have been set up for rest, along with a bookshelf that invites visitors to read and exchange books.
