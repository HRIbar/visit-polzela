The current appearance of Štober House reflects its renovation in 1841. The year is carved into the stone portal made from green tuff. It is classified as a house type with an original black kitchen and a central Slovenian room layout. Its former social status is reflected in its richly articulated facade. The entire homestead has significant monumental value.

Trenutni videz Štoberjeve hiše odraža njeno prenovo leta 1841. Leto je vklesano v kamniti portal iz zelenega tufa. Hiša je razvrščena kot tip hiše z originalno črno kuhinjo in osrednjo slovensko razporeditvijo prostorov. Njena nekdanja družbena statusa se odražata v bogato oblikovani fasadi. Celotna domačija ima pomembno monumentalno vrednost.
